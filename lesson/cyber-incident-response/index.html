Incident Response Lifecycle

Définition: La réponse à incident couvre préparation, détection, confinement, éradication, récupération et retour d’expérience.

Kreyòl: IR se plan aksyon pou limite domaj lè gen atak.

Why it matters

Speed and coordination determine business impact during a breach.

Core concepts

  • Triage severity
  • Containment strategy
  • Postmortem

Deep explanation

Good IR includes playbooks, communication trees, forensic readiness, and post-incident corrective actions.

Step-by-step breakdown

  1. Prepare playbooks.
  2. Detect and triage alerts.
  3. Contain affected systems.
  4. Eradicate root cause.
  5. Recover services.
  6. Conduct lessons learned.

Code and commands (English)

# Capture active connections during triage
ss -tulpen

# Collect process list
ps aux --sort=-%cpu | head

Erreurs fréquentes

  • No legal/comms coordination
  • Destroying evidence too early
